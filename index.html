About Me

A Brief Biography

Auttasit Ruangsilp is a distinguished criminology and criminal justice researcher with over 10 years of experience in the field. He holds a Ph.D. in Criminology from a prestigious university and specializes in policing reform, juvenile justice, and evidence-based crime prevention strategies. His research focuses on bridging the gap between academic theory and practical application in criminal justice policy. Ruangsilp has published numerous peer-reviewed articles and frequently consults with government agencies on criminal justice reform initiatives. His work is dedicated to advancing justice through rigorous research and data-driven insights.
